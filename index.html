What We Do

Community Service

We give back to the community through STEM events and the mentoring of younger students!

Competition

We travel all along the Northeast to participate in collegiate invitationals. We are top contenders in the State competition.

Learning & Building

Science Olympiad is oriented around learning and building - as a club, we offer engaging experiences into new fields from ecology to engineering to all our members.

More About Us

Who Are We?

We are a collection of dedicated, driven, and passionate students at Montgomery High School. Some of us have dedicated all four years of our high school career to the club, building and studying countless late nights.

Why Donate?

By donating to Science Olympiad, you are contributing to the life-source of the club. We depend on these funds to purchase necessary building materials and tools, attend competitions, and to run our service initiatives. We would greatly appreciate a donation in any shape or form!

Accolades

In the past years, the Montgomery Science Olympiad Team has placed in the top 3 at the State competition. In addition, we've previously placed first at the Cornell, Yale, and Princeton collegiate invitationals, and in the top 20 for the MIT and Penn invitationals.
